RECT Structure

The RECT structure defines the coordinates of the upper-left and lower-right
corners of a rectangle.

   [Member]  [Type]   [32bitA]  [32bitU]  [64bitU]
1  left      LONG     [00] 4    [00] 4    [00] 4
2  top       LONG     [04] 4    [04] 4    [04] 4
3  right     LONG     [08] 4    [08] 4    [08] 4
4  bottom    LONG     [12] 4    [12] 4    [12] 4
                           ---       ---       ---
                           16        16        16
