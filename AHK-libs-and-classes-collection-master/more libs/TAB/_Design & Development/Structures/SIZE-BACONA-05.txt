SIZE structure

The SIZE structure specifies the width and height of a rectangle.

   [Member]  [Type]   [32bitA]  [32bitU]  [64bitU]
1  cx        LONG     [0] 4     [0] 4     [0] 4
2  cy        LONG     [4] 4     [4] 4     [4] 4
                          --        --        --
                          8         8         8
