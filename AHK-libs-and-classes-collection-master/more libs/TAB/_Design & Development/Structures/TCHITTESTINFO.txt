TCHITTESTINFO structure

Contains information about a hit test. This structure supersedes the
TC_HITTESTINFO structure.

   [Member]  [Type]   [32bitA]  [32bitU]  [64bitU]
1  pt.x      int      [00] 4    [00] 4    [00] 4
2  pt.y      int      [04] 4    [04] 4    [04] 4
3  flags     UINT     [08] 4    [08] 4    [08] 4
                           --        --        --
                           12        12        12
